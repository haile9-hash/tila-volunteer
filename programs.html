Our Ongoing Programs

Blood Donation Campaign

Join us in saving lives by donating blood. Our regular campaigns help replenish blood supplies and support patients in need.

Books for Children Collection

Help us collect books to provide young children with the resources they need to learn and grow. Every book matters!

Building Houses for Elders

We aim to build homes for the elderly who are in need of safe, secure shelter. Your support can make a difference!

Exhibition Preparation

We organize community exhibitions to showcase local talents, arts, and cultural heritage. Come be part of this vibrant initiative!

Street Children Support

We provide assistance to street children, helping them access education, healthcare, and safe environments. Your support can change lives!

Life Skills Training

Empowering individuals with essential life skills such as financial literacy, health education, and conflict resolution.
