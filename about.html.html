Who we are

Tila Voluntary Association founded in 2012 by 15 students in Bahir Dar city administration. The voluntary Association has been fulfilling its social responsibility by providing educational materials, clothing, monthly allowance and other support.

Tila voluntary Association, which is in its 5th year, is currently doing great work with not less than 60 young people as members. It is a voluntary association that has been working since June 2015 by signing a memorandum of understanding with peer associations

Vision

To see the nature of a generation that is socially, economically and psychologically developed , free from the spirit of old age (needs absolute support from the person) and useful to itself and society.

Objective

✓ Creating an active citizen and patriotic society in our city by the year of 2025
✓ Creating a young person who protects himself, his family and friends from addiction and gender violence
✓ Creating job opportunities for young people and the elderly to support themselves and their families in 2025.
✓ To increase the number of volunteers in Bahir Dar city by 15 percent in 2025 from current data.
✓ Enhancing Ethiopian good values that increase Ethiopian identity, national unity and social cohesion through training and exchange of experiences.

Major Focusing Area

✓ Health, Education, Economy, Capacity ...
✓ Health (SRH, Gender based violence, harmful practice, early marriage, prevention of HIV/AIDS)
✓ Education ( supporting student who are not fulfilling their education material)
✓ Economy (enhancing youth economic empower by creating job opportunities )
✓ Capacity building (facilitating capacity building training for youths and their family)

Detailed Tasks

✓ Providing support to street children
